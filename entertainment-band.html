The Entertainment Band & Friends Bio

If you want to hear Top 40's popular music you simply turn on the radio, but if you want to see the Classics done right there is no other band but The Entertainment Band & Friends! The Entertainment Band & Friends are a solo to 12 piece band from Atlanta, Ga that plays all your Funk Jazz Soul R&B classics and then some. Since playing over 30 years The Entertainment Band & Friends has performed at nearly 600 events and festivals. They are a staple in the diets of thousands of music-lovers that request the band for Corporate Events to Private Party’s thanks to clients such as; The Entertainment Venue, Legendary Events, Chateau Elan, The City Winery, City Of Powder Springs, 5 Church, Atlanta’s very own Greenbay Packers “Preston Smith’s” Wedding and Reception, Lockheed Martin, 3M, 4th of July Festivals for the State, AKA, Deltas, City of Atlanta and much more.


The band’s sound fuses heavy-hitting dance grooves with flashy horn lines, energetic instrumental solos and soaring vocals that will make you want to dance all night. “On stage, every single member is equally strong in their musicality and their delivery. This kind of chemistry from a band is a real, rare treat.”-Legendary Events


As longtime friends, collaborators, and multi-dimensional artists, Clarence Pennie the founder of The Entertainment Band & Friends are a creative talented group of artists “friends” that blend various genres and produce a chemistry of music that is perfectly aligned for any size/style crowd or event.


The Entertainment Band & Friends combine their powers as DJs, sound engineers, and musicians resulting in a brilliant technical display of creative collaboration for any special event.


The Entertainment Band & Friends enjoy a vibrant touring career with major festival plays for private clients such as; Legendary Events, The City Winery, City Of Powder Springs, 5 Church, Atlanta’s own Greenbay Packers “Preston Smith” Wedding and Reception, Chateau Elan, Lockheed Martin, 3M, 4th of July Festivals for the State, AKA, Deltas, Omegas , City of Atlanta and much more.


Did you know that artists such as Bruno Mars, Justin Timberlake, Beyonce and Megan Trainer sampled a lot of their materials from the Old School? Hear those soulful vocals? The catchy horn lines? The drum and bass with heavy backbeats? That's the Old School! We pride ourselves in knowing a wide range of repertoire: Something from the Old School and something from the New School, but always something to keep everybody happy!


We invite you to explore live music for your next intimate to large event and witness the energy of the room transform the guests memories for years to come.
